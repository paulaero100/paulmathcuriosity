Why this matters
The Setup
A double pendulum is two pendulums attached end-to-end. Simple classical mechanics — completely deterministic, governed by Newton's laws. No randomness anywhere in the system.
The Mystery
Start two identical double pendulums just 0.001° apart. Within seconds they are in completely different states. This is the butterfly effect, mathematically exact.
The Implication
Weather prediction fails past ~10 days not because our computers are too slow, but because the atmosphere is a chaotic system. Perfect prediction requires infinite precision — physically impossible.
